BurnerPad

Privacy Policy · Last updated: March 2026

Overview

BurnerPad is a local, encrypted notepad application for Android. It is designed from the ground up to collect no data about you. Your notes never leave your device.

Data we collect

None. BurnerPad does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry. There are no servers, no accounts, and no network connections made by this application.

Your notes

All notes are stored exclusively on your device, encrypted using AES-256-CBC with a key derived from your PIN via PBKDF2WithHmacSHA256. Notes are never uploaded, synced, backed up to any cloud service, or transmitted in any form. Backup archives created within the app are saved to your device's Downloads folder and are encrypted — they are never sent anywhere automatically.

Third-party services

BurnerPad does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, analytics platforms, or crash reporting tools.

Permissions

BurnerPad requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality: storage access for importing and exporting files. No other permissions are requested or used.

Open source

BurnerPad is fully open source. You can inspect the complete source code at github.com/Zaegan/BurnerPad.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the GitHub repository linked above.
